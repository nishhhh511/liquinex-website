ABOUT US

Our Capabilities & Story

Pioneering robust, sustainable, and chemical-free water purification systems since 2000.

Our Purpose

Providing clean water sustainably for municipal, disaster relief, and industrial sectors.

Singapore Engineering Excellence

Compact & Robust Engineering

Liquinex designs and manufactures compact, containerized, and portable water treatment systems using advanced ceramic membrane technology. We replace chemical-heavy processes with physical filtration and inline UVC disinfection, reducing operations and maintenance footprints.

Milestones

Corporate Journey

How we pioneered new water treatment solutions across Southeast Asia and globally.

2000

Inception & Focus

Founded with the core goal of developing compact, chemical-free, and portable water filtration units for humanitarian disaster relief deployments and rural municipal sites.

2013

Riaz Deen Join Date

Incorporated new engineering frameworks under Riaz Deen. Transitioned research pipelines to focus on high-performance Silicon Carbide (SiC) membranes, launching industrial product skids.

2017

Southeast Asia Distribution

Signed regional partnerships for shipping container-sized WaterWall and WaterPack systems, deploying water treatment units to municipal sites across Indonesia and Malaysia.

2020

Global Water Awards

Awarded international recognition in Dubai for our humanitarian disaster relief suitcase system, supplying potable water systems to remote clinics and Red Cross stations.
