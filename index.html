Our Capabilities

Established in 2015, Liquinex has grown from a specialized water technology developer into a premier, integrated Engineering Procurement Construction (EPC) and Operations & Maintenance (O&M) solutions provider. We specialize in the custom engineering, fabrication, and systems integration of compact, highly efficient water and wastewater treatment platforms.


By leveraging next-generation advanced membrane filtration—including silicon carbide ceramic, biomimetic, and graphene membranes—coupled with advanced physical oxidation processes such as Deep UVC disinfection and titanium oxide catalysts, we deliver robust, eco-friendly water purification systems. Our proven track record ranges from ultra-compact, solar-powered humanitarian water suitcases for disaster relief to massive, automated industrial recycling systems for multinational corporations.

Featured Case Studies

MUNICIPAL PLANT

Lembaga Air Perak River Treatment

A high-efficiency municipal water treatment plant deployed to draw raw water from the Sungei Siput River and supply high-quality potable drinking water to the rural community of Kampung Perlop in Perak, Malaysia.

Challenge: Severe seasonal turbidity and organic loading in the river water that rendered standard sand filters inoperable.

Solution: Installed a compact, fully automated ultrafiltration (UF) system utilizing robust hollow-fiber membrane modules.

Outcome: Delivers 200,000 liters of safe, clean drinking water per day directly to consumers, fully compliant with national potability metrics.

AQUACULTURE SYSTEM

Singapore Zoo Recirculating RAS

A specialized Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS) deployed at the River Safari at Singapore Zoo to filter fish tank effluent, excrement, and algae blooms, replacing space-consuming sand filters.

Challenge: Heavy fish fecal loading, algae blooms, and organic waste causing high turbidity and clogging in traditional sand filters, requiring significant maintenance and water waste.

Solution: Installed a custom compact membrane filtration loop with physical anti-fouling backwashing cycles.

Outcome: Dramatically lowered backwash water loss, eliminated chemical dosing, reduced floor space usage, and reduced manual labor. Drag the visual slider to inspect water clarity!

INDUSTRIAL RECYCLING

REC Cooling Tower DBOO

A 5+5 year Design-Build-Own-Operate (DBOO) water-as-a-service plant delivering processed, recycled water to REC Solar from cooling tower blowdown wastewater.

Challenge: Heavy industrial wastewater loading containing suspended silica and chemical antiscalants that fouled organic membranes.

Solution: Deployed a containerized, automated silicon carbide ceramic membrane plant operating on direct filtration metrics.

Outcome: Recycles up to 40 m³/hr of wastewater 24/7, supplying it back to REC Solar at a rate of $1.35/m³, yielding direct OPEX savings without initial capital expenditure.
REC Solar Group
Tuas Industrial Estate, Singapore

Zero Waste Water

Sustainability at Our Core

At Liquinex, sustainability is not just a goal; it is our engineering core. We actively contribute to the circular economy by recycling End-of-Life (EOL) membranes from municipal water agencies like Singapore's PUB, refurbishing them for secondary industrial applications.

By replacing chemical-heavy water treatment with chemical-free ceramic filtration and physical UVC sterilization, we minimize secondary pollution and chemical discharge. Our systems are engineered to optimize energy efficiency, reducing operational carbon footprints and delivering cost-effective water security to vulnerable and remote communities.
