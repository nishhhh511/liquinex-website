Who We Are

About Liquinex

Our Capabilities

Established in 2015, Liquinex has grown from a specialized water technology developer into a premier, integrated Engineering Procurement Construction (EPC) and Operations & Maintenance (O&M) solutions provider. We specialize in the custom engineering, fabrication, and systems integration of compact, highly efficient water and wastewater treatment platforms.

By leveraging next-generation advanced membrane filtration—including silicon carbide ceramic, biomimetic, and graphene membranes—coupled with advanced physical oxidation processes such as Deep UVC disinfection and titanium oxide catalysts, we deliver robust, eco-friendly water purification systems. Our proven track record ranges from ultra-compact, solar-powered humanitarian water suitcases for disaster relief to massive, automated industrial recycling systems for multinational corporations.

Circular Economy

Sustainability at Our Core

Refurbishing End-of-Life Membranes

At Liquinex, sustainability is not just a goal; it is our engineering core. We actively contribute to the circular economy by recycling End-of-Life (EOL) membranes from municipal water agencies like Singapore's PUB, refurbishing them for secondary industrial applications.

By replacing chemical-heavy water treatment with chemical-free ceramic filtration and physical UVC sterilization, we minimize secondary pollution and chemical discharge. Our systems are engineered to optimize energy efficiency, reducing operational carbon footprints and delivering cost-effective water security to vulnerable and remote communities.
