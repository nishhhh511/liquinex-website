About Liquinex

Engineering Better
Water Solutions

Founded in 2015, Liquinex is a Singapore-based specialist in water and wastewater treatment technologies. We design, fabricate, and integrate compact, high-performance systems using advanced membrane, UV, and oxidation technologies.

Our solutions serve industries, municipalities, and disaster relief communities with a focus on innovation, efficiency, and sustainability.
